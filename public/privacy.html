Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  Applies to residents of the European Union (GDPR)

This Privacy Policy describes how The Barbershop ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our online booking service at this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Greek law.

If you have any questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at the details above.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to process your appointment booking:

  • Full name — to identify your booking.
  • Phone number — to send a one-time SMS verification code and to contact you about your appointment.
  • Email address (only if you use Facebook Login) — provided by Facebook as part of your public profile.
  • Facebook profile name and user ID (only if you use Facebook Login) — used to create your booking.
  • Booking details — service chosen, date, time, and price.

We do not collect payment card details, passwords, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To fulfil the booking contract — processing and confirming your appointment (legal basis: contract performance, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • To verify your identity — sending a one-time SMS code to your phone (legal basis: contract performance).
  • To manage our schedule — preventing double-bookings and organising our work calendar (legal basis: legitimate interests, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • To contact you — in case of cancellation or schedule changes (legal basis: legitimate interests).

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Vonage (Ericsson) — used to send SMS verification codes. Your phone number is transmitted to Vonage solely for this purpose. Vonage is an EU-compliant processor. See their privacy policy at vonage.com.
  • Meta (Facebook Login) — if you choose to verify with Facebook, we receive your name and email from Meta. This is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy at facebook.com/privacy. We do not store your Facebook password or access token.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Booking records (name, phone, service, date/time) — retained for 2 years for business record-keeping purposes, then permanently deleted.
  • SMS verification codes — deleted immediately after use or after 10 minutes if unused.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data, unless we are legally required to retain it.
  • Right to restriction — request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@thebarbershop.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic Data Protection Authority (HDPA) at www.dpa.gr.

7. Cookies

Our booking page uses a session cookie set by the Facebook Login SDK (only active if you initiate Facebook Login). This cookie is used solely for authentication and is not used for tracking or advertising. No other cookies are set by this website.

8. Data Security

All data is stored locally on our server and is not transmitted to cloud databases. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information. Access to booking data is restricted to the barbershop owner via a password-protected admin panel.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our booking service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
